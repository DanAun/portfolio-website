About Me

Hi, my name is Daniel Aunan!

I'm a cybersecurity professional with a double master's degree in Computer Engineering and Cybersecurity & Information Systems Security from INSA Toulouse and the Toulouse-Security (TLS-SEC) program. My academic journey has equipped me with a robust foundation in computer science and a specialized focus on safeguarding systems and data against cyber threats.

Currently, I'm a Cybersecurity Trainee at EUROCONTROL's European Air Traffic Management Computer Emergency Response Team (EATM-CERT) in Brussels. In this role, I engage in penetration testing of Azure cloud infrastructures for aviation stakeholders, develop Capture The Flag (CTF) challenges to enhance cybersecurity skills within the aviation community, and participate in workshops exploring the intersection of AI and cybersecurity metrics.

My engineering background fosters a problem-solving mindset, enabling me to tackle complex challenges with innovative solutions. I'm passionate about exploring new technologies and continuously expanding my knowledge to stay ahead in the ever-evolving field of cybersecurity.
